About US:

Beyond, an upcoming digital marketing agency, our team has an aggregated 30+ years of experienced in digital marketing, customer excellence, business acquisition and event management, launched in Jan 2016, throughout last year we have worked with prestigious private and governmental clients that shaped our vision and made us take steady steps, by end of 2017 we are planning to be operational remotely in 4 other countries within the gulf region besides our hometown Egypt.

Our services include but not limited to Digital Marketing, Content Creation, Printing & Advertising, Brand identity, concept creation, event management & sponsorship deals.

Currently, we have 15+ Clients for Digital, Offline & Event Management and marketings in the fields of Food & Beverage, Entertainment, Nightlife, Online Shopping, Fashion & Exhibitions.

Our main target currently is to provide an excellent combination of digital services at a very reasonable price to allow owners and managers of businesses " Small & Medium " to enhance their product/service showcase experience without paying an overrated price.
